About India

  • India is a land of rich traditions, vibrant festivals, and diverse landscapes. From the majestic Himalayas to the serene backwaters of Kerala, every corner tells a story worth discovering.

    • Top Destinations

    Agra

    Home to the iconic Taj Mahal, a wonder of the world and symbol of love.

    Jaipur

    The Pink City with royal palaces, forts, and vibrant culture.

    Goa

    Famous for beaches, nightlife, and Portuguese heritage.

    Kerala

    God’s Own Country with backwaters, ayurveda, and lush greenery.

    Varanasi

    India’s spiritual capital with ancient temples and Ganga aartis.

    Ladakh

    Adventure paradise with monasteries, mountains, and clear lakes.

    Mumbai

    Known for palaces, Dasara festival, and traditional silk.

    Kashmir

    The City of Joy with colonial charm, literature, and sweets.

    Experience Indian Culture

    Festivals

    Celebrate Diwali, Holi, and Navratri with vibrant traditions.

    Spirituality

    Visit temples, ghats, and explore yoga & meditation.

    Cuisine

    Delight in regional thalis, spices, and sweet desserts.

    Art & Dance

    Enjoy classical forms like Kathak and Bharatnatyam.

    Essential Travel Tips

    • Carry copies of your passport, visa, and travel insurance.
    • Use prepaid taxis or apps like Uber/Ola for safe transport.
    • Drink only bottled water and eat at recommended places.
    • Respect local customs, dress modestly at religious sites.
    • Learn a few Hindi phrases – locals appreciate it!

    Sample 7-Day Itinerary

    Day 1: Delhi

    Explore Red Fort, Qutub Minar, and street food in Chandni Chowk.

    Day 2–3: Agra & Jaipur

    Visit the Taj Mahal, Fatehpur Sikri, then head to Jaipur's Amber Fort and Hawa Mahal.

    Day 4–5: Varanasi

    Attend the Ganga Aarti, take a boat ride at sunrise, and visit Sarnath.

    Day 6–7: Kerala

    Relax on houseboats, enjoy an ayurvedic massage, and try coconut-based cuisine.
